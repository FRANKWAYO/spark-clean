About us

Started with one mop, one van, and a promise.

SparkClean Services began in 2017 when our founder, tired of inconsistent cleaning crews, decided to build the reliable service she couldn't find. Today we run a trained, background-checked team serving homes, offices, and job sites across the region.

Every clean follows the same checklist, uses the same graded supplies, and is backed by the same guarantee — whether it's a studio apartment or a 40,000 sq ft warehouse.
